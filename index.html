🏠 Our Story

Started with a Mother's Recipe, Grown with Love

EatsYummy started in 2021 with a simple mission — bring the warmth of home-cooked food to working professionals, students, and families in Pune who miss ghar ka khana.

Today we serve 500+ happy customers daily with four thoughtfully designed tiffin plans — Regular, High Protein, Jain (no onion/garlic), and fully customizable Patient-friendly meals.

Every tiffin is packed fresh, on time, with love — because food is not just fuel, it's comfort. No preservatives, no artificial flavours — just pure, honest cooking.
